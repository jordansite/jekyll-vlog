About Me.





When I was in high school, I had no idea who I was or what I wanted to do.
I was lost inside my mind, I didn’t understand why I wasn’t popular. I didn’t know why people didn’t want to be around me.
I would try to talk to people or message them with no luck. I had some friends but most weekends were spent at home watching TV or playing video games. I have a distinct memory in my head of my dad always asking me what I was doing this weekend. My answer would always be the same, nothing much just staying in. That was me 5 years ago, since then I have struggle and worked hard to achieve great success.

I have been on an uphill battle to be the best that I can be. I have achieved success in school, work, friends, productivity, fitness and travel. I had some failures along my journey. I went to school to be a mechanical engineer and dropped out because I loved snowboarding more than I loved going to class. Why go waste my time at school and not learn because I didn’t enjoy it? When instead I could go snowboard and love every minute of it! I had many lonely days on the hill but I was always working hard to become a better snowboarder. I decided to take a year off and find a way to have an income by working at home. I was doing well until about a month into it I got a job working in an office. This was a great learning experience which then motivated me to go back to school.

I went to college for 2 years and received a Wind Turbine Technician Diploma with Honours.
I graduated at the top of my class and received 3 job offers. I then chose the best one suited to my needs.
All of that wasn’t enough for me. I worked hard and achieved great success in my field but I wanted more.
I wanted to help people and work for myself, not someone else. I am not a slave driver to make money for someone I don’t even know.
I don’t accept what is, I am always looking for something better.
That is why you are here, you are looking to better yourself.
I am here to help you do just that. Through my writings and videos I will show you how I have achieved so much, at such a young age.
You will be able to see exactly how I live my life and better myself on a weekly basis.
